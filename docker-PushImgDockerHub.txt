Pushing an image to DockerHub on the Internet.
To push a working image to DockerHub on the Internet for others to download.

$ cd ~/my-simple-webapp
$ docker build . -t highwind01/my-simple-webapp
$ docker login
Username: highwind01
Password: P********1
$ docker push highwind01/my-simple-webapp

Then go to https://hub.docker.com, login as highwind01 and look in the Repositories.
